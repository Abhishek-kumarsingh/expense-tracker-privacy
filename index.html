Privacy Policy

Expense Tracker  ·  Last updated: May 1, 2026

Finance App · Android

This Privacy Policy describes how Expense Tracker ("the App", "we", "us") collects, uses, and protects information when you use our Android application (com.encrypting.expensetracker2).

By using the App you agree to the collection and use of information as described in this policy. If you do not agree, please do not use the App.

1. Information We Collect

We collect only what is necessary to provide the App's features:

  • Account information — your email address when you sign up or sign in. We use Supabase for authentication; your credentials are securely stored by Supabase.
  • Expense data — amounts, merchant names, categories, dates, and notes that you enter manually or import from shared UPI receipts. This data is stored in your Supabase account database and is associated with your user ID.
  • Device token — a Firebase Cloud Messaging (FCM) token used to deliver push notifications (e.g., recurring expense reminders). This token identifies your device, not you personally.

We do not collect your name, phone number, location, contacts, camera roll, payment card details, or any other personal data beyond the above.

2. How We Use Your Information

  • To authenticate you and keep your data private to your account.
  • To store and sync your expense records across sessions.
  • To send you push notifications for recurring expense reminders (if enabled).
  • To pre-fill expense forms when you share a UPI receipt into the App (on-device parsing only — the receipt text is never sent to any server).

We do not use your data for advertising, profiling, or any purpose beyond operating the App.

3. Data Sharing

We do not sell, rent, or share your personal data with third parties, except:

  • Supabase — our backend provider for authentication and database storage. Supabase processes data under their own Privacy Policy.
  • Google Firebase (FCM) — used solely for push notification delivery. Firebase processes data under Google's Privacy Policy.
  • Legal obligations — we may disclose data if required by law or to protect the rights and safety of users.

4. On-Device Processing

The UPI receipt parsing feature (Share-to-App intake) processes receipt text entirely on your device using a bundled model. No receipt text or image is transmitted to any external server or API. The extracted data (amount, merchant, date) is used only to pre-fill the expense form and is not stored separately.

5. Data Retention

Your expense data is retained in your Supabase account for as long as you use the App. You can delete individual expenses at any time within the App. To delete your account and all associated data, contact us at alpsingh03@gmail.com and we will process the deletion within 30 days.

6. Data Security

Data in transit between the App and Supabase is encrypted via HTTPS/TLS. Data at rest is secured by Supabase's infrastructure (row-level security policies ensure each user can only access their own records). We do not store credentials or sensitive data on the device beyond the authentication session token.

7. Children's Privacy

The App is not directed at children under the age of 13. We do not knowingly collect personal data from children under 13. If you believe we have inadvertently collected such data, please contact us immediately so we can delete it.

8. Your Rights

You have the right to:

  • Access the personal data we hold about you.
  • Request correction of inaccurate data.
  • Request deletion of your data.
  • Withdraw consent at any time by deleting the App.

To exercise any of these rights, contact us at alpsingh03@gmail.com.

9. Changes to This Policy
