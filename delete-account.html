What data will be deleted

  • Your account credentials (email and password)
  • All expense records you have entered
  • Budget settings and category data
  • Recurring expense configurations
  • Push notification token (FCM device token)

Timeline

We will confirm receipt of your request within 5 business days and complete the deletion within 30 days.

Retention exceptions

We may retain certain information longer if required by law. No financial transaction data is shared with third parties before deletion.
